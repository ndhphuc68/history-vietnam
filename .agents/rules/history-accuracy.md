# Historical Accuracy Rules

## Vietnamese History Learning App

This project teaches Vietnamese history to children.

Historical accuracy is extremely important.

AI must never invent historical facts.

---

# 1. Accuracy Principle

All historical content must be:

- factually correct
- historically verified
- simplified but not distorted

If unsure about a fact, AI must say:

"Historical sources differ."

Never guess.

---

# 2. Trusted Historical Sources

Prefer knowledge from widely accepted historical records.

Examples:

- Vietnamese history textbooks
- historical research
- national museum sources

Avoid:

- fictional stories
- myths presented as facts

Legends must be clearly labeled as legends.

---

# 3. Historical Figures

Information about historical figures must include:

- correct name
- correct historical period
- correct achievements

Example:

Ngô Quyền
Victory at the Battle of Bạch Đằng
Year: 938

AI must never assign achievements to the wrong person.

---

# 4. Historical Events

Each event must include:

- correct year (or approximate period)
- correct location
- correct participants
- correct outcome

Example:

Battle of Bạch Đằng
Year: 938
Leader: Ngô Quyền
Result: Defeat of the Southern Han fleet

---

# 5. Timeline Consistency

Events must follow correct chronological order.

Example:

Ngô Quyền → 10th century
Lý dynasty → 11th century
Trần dynasty → 13th century

AI must not mix timelines.

---

# 6. Simplification Rules

Content must be simplified for children.

However:

Simplification must never change the core historical fact.

Example:

Correct:

Ngô Quyền defeated the Southern Han fleet.

Incorrect:

Ngô Quyền defeated the Chinese empire.

---

# 7. Avoid Political Bias

Historical content must remain neutral.

Avoid:

- modern political framing
- ideological commentary

Focus on historical facts.

---

# 8. Historical Legends

Vietnamese history includes many legends.

Examples:

- Thánh Gióng
- Sơn Tinh – Thủy Tinh
- Lạc Long Quân – Âu Cơ

These must be labeled clearly:

Legend

Do not present legends as verified historical events.

---

# 9. Fact Verification Rule

Before generating lesson content AI must:

1. Identify the historical event
2. Confirm the historical figure
3. Confirm the correct time period

Then generate content.

---

# 10. Conflict Resolution

If historical sources disagree:

AI must say:

"Historians have different interpretations."

Do not choose a random answer.

---

# 11. Quiz Accuracy

Quiz questions must only test:

- verified facts
- widely accepted historical information

Avoid controversial topics.

---

# 12. Language Rules

When explaining history:

Use clear language.

Avoid:

- exaggeration
- speculation
- fictional storytelling

Focus on real historical events.

---

# 13. Respect Historical Context

Do not judge historical figures using modern standards.

Explain actions within their historical context.

---

# 14. Content Review Principle

All generated lessons must prioritize:

accuracy over creativity.
