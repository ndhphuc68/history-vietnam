# Vietnamese History Learning Order

## Learning Progression for Children

This project teaches Vietnamese history following a clear storyline:

Dựng nước → Giữ nước

Lessons must follow chronological order.

AI must generate lessons following this order.

---

# 1. Founding the Nation (Dựng Nước)

These lessons introduce the origins of the Vietnamese people.

Important figures:

Hùng Vương
An Dương Vương

Key topics:

Origin of Văn Lang
Âu Lạc kingdom
Cổ Loa citadel

Purpose:

Explain how the Vietnamese nation began.

---

# 2. Early Resistance (Chống Bắc Thuộc)

Vietnam was under foreign domination for many centuries.

Important figures:

Hai Bà Trưng
Bà Triệu
Lý Bí

Key events:

Trưng Sisters uprising (40 AD)
Lady Triệu rebellion (248 AD)
Lý Bí establishes Vạn Xuân kingdom

Purpose:

Show early struggles for independence.

---

# 3. Independence Restored

Important figure:

Ngô Quyền

Key event:

Battle of Bạch Đằng (938)

Outcome:

Vietnam regained independence after nearly 1000 years.

---

# 4. Building the Nation

Important dynasties:

Đinh dynasty
Early Lê dynasty
Lý dynasty

Important figures:

Đinh Bộ Lĩnh
Lê Hoàn
Lý Thái Tổ
Lý Thường Kiệt

Important events:

Unifying the country
Moving the capital to Thăng Long
War against the Song dynasty

Purpose:

Explain how the Vietnamese state became stable and strong.

---

# 5. Defending the Nation

Important dynasty:

Trần dynasty

Important figures:

Trần Hưng Đạo
Trần Nhân Tông

Important events:

Mongol invasions of Vietnam

Key battles:

Battle of Bạch Đằng (1288)

Purpose:

Show how Vietnam defended itself against powerful invaders.

---

# 6. Restoring Independence Again

Important figure:

Lê Lợi

Important event:

Lam Sơn uprising

Outcome:

Vietnam gained independence from Ming occupation.

Important supporting figure:

Nguyễn Trãi

---

# 7. Heroic Victory Against Qing

Important figure:

Nguyễn Huệ (Quang Trung)

Important event:

Battle of Ngọc Hồi – Đống Đa (1789)

Outcome:

Defeat of Qing army.

---

# 8. Colonial Period

Important context:

French colonization of Vietnam.

Important movements:

Cần Vương movement
Early resistance movements

Purpose:

Explain challenges during colonial rule.

---

# 9. Independence Movement

Important figure:

Hồ Chí Minh

Important event:

Declaration of Independence (1945)

Purpose:

Vietnam declares independence.

---

# 10. Resistance War Against France

Time:

1945 – 1954

Important event:

Battle of Điện Biên Phủ (1954)

Outcome:

End of French colonial rule.

---

# 11. Resistance War Against the United States

Time:

1955 – 1975

Important events:

Tet Offensive
Ho Chi Minh Campaign

Outcome:

Reunification of Vietnam in 1975.

---

# 12. Lesson Order Rule

Lessons must follow this order:

1 Founding the Nation
2 Early Resistance
3 Independence Restored
4 Building the Nation
5 Defending the Nation
6 Restoring Independence
7 Victory Against Qing
8 Colonial Period
9 Independence Movement
10 War Against France
11 War Against the United States

AI must not skip historical progression.

---

# 13. Educational Goal

The goal of this learning path is to help children understand:

how Vietnam was founded
how Vietnam defended its independence
how Vietnam achieved reunification.

History should be presented as a continuous story.
