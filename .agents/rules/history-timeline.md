# Vietnamese History Timeline

## Reference Timeline for AI

This document defines the main timeline of Vietnamese history.

AI must use this timeline when generating lessons.

Historical figures and events must match the correct period.

---

# 1. Ancient Period

Time: before 111 BC

Key states:

Văn Lang
Âu Lạc

Important figures:

Hùng Kings (Hùng Vương)
An Dương Vương

Important events:

Foundation of Văn Lang
Creation of Cổ Loa citadel

---

# 2. Chinese Domination Period

Time: 111 BC – 939 AD

Vietnam was under Chinese rule for nearly 1000 years.

Important rebellions:

Trưng Sisters (40 AD)
Lady Triệu (248 AD)
Lý Bí (544 AD)

Key figure:

Ngô Quyền

Important event:

Battle of Bạch Đằng (938)

Result:

End of Chinese domination.

---

# 3. Early Independent Period

Time: 939 – 1009

Key leaders:

Ngô Quyền
Đinh Bộ Lĩnh
Lê Hoàn

Important events:

Establishment of independent rule
Formation of Đại Cồ Việt

---

# 4. Lý Dynasty

Time: 1009 – 1225

Key ruler:

Lý Thái Tổ

Important events:

Capital moved to Thăng Long (1010)

Important figures:

Lý Thường Kiệt

Important battles:

War against Song Dynasty (1075–1077)

---

# 5. Trần Dynasty

Time: 1225 – 1400

Important figures:

Trần Hưng Đạo
Trần Thánh Tông
Trần Nhân Tông

Important events:

Mongol invasions of Vietnam

Important battles:

Battle of Bạch Đằng (1288)

Vietnam defeated Mongol forces.

---

# 6. Hồ Dynasty

Time: 1400 – 1407

Key figure:

Hồ Quý Ly

Important event:

Short-lived dynasty before Ming invasion.

---

# 7. Ming Occupation

Time: 1407 – 1427

Vietnam under Ming rule.

Important rebellion:

Lam Sơn uprising.

Key figure:

Lê Lợi

---

# 8. Lê Dynasty

Time: 1428 – 1789

Key ruler:

Lê Thái Tổ (Lê Lợi)

Important achievements:

Restoration of Vietnamese independence.

Important figures:

Nguyễn Trãi
Lê Thánh Tông

---

# 9. Tây Sơn Period

Time: 1771 – 1802

Important figures:

Nguyễn Huệ (Quang Trung)

Important events:

Defeat of Qing army (1789)

Important battle:

Battle of Ngọc Hồi – Đống Đa.

---

# 10. Nguyễn Dynasty

Time: 1802 – 1945

Founder:

Nguyễn Ánh (Gia Long)

Important characteristics:

Last imperial dynasty of Vietnam.

Important events:

French colonization of Vietnam.

---

# 11. Modern Period

Time: 1945 – Present

Important event:

Declaration of Independence (1945)

Key figure:

Hồ Chí Minh

Important events:

Vietnam War
Reunification of Vietnam (1975)

---

# 12. Timeline Validation Rule

When generating historical lessons AI must:

1. Identify the correct historical period
2. Identify the correct dynasty
3. Confirm the historical figure belongs to that period

Example:

Ngô Quyền → Chinese Domination Period ending → year 938

Trần Hưng Đạo → Trần Dynasty → 13th century

Nguyễn Huệ → Tây Sơn Period → 18th century

---

# 13. Avoid Timeline Confusion

AI must never mix historical periods.

Examples of mistakes to avoid:

Ngô Quyền in Trần dynasty
Trần Hưng Đạo in Lý dynasty
Nguyễn Huệ in Lê dynasty

---

# 14. Lesson Creation Rule

When creating lessons AI must follow this process:

1. Identify historical period
2. Identify key figure
3. Identify key event
4. Generate lesson slides
5. Generate quiz

---

# 15. Priority Periods for Children

Lessons should prioritize famous events:

Battle of Bạch Đằng (938)
Trưng Sisters uprising
Trần Hưng Đạo vs Mongols
Lê Lợi and Lam Sơn uprising
Quang Trung defeating Qing army

These events are easier for children to understand.
