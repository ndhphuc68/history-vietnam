# Visual Style Guide: Vietnamese History Learning App

To ensure all images in the application feel like they belong to the same "world," please follow this style guide for all image generation.

## 🎨 Color Palette
- **Primary:** Sunlit Gold, Deep Emerald Green, Rich Crimson, Ocean Blue.
- **Secondary:** Warm Wood, Earthy Brown, Soft Sky Blue.
- **Vibe:** Highly saturated but with soft transitions. Use harmonious colors to create a friendly, storybook atmosphere.

## 🖌️ Design & Artistic Style
- **Type:** Children's book illustration (flat vector style).
- **Lines:** Clean, well-defined outlines (not too thick).
- **Shading:** Simple, soft gradients. No complex or realistic lighting.
- **Backgrounds:** Simplified, symbolic rather than detailed. Focus on the main action.

## 👤 Characters
- **Design:** Simplified facial features (friendly expressions, dots for eyes).
- **Proportions:** Consistent across different lessons.
- **Garments:** Simplified traditional Vietnamese clothing (Ao Dai, Ao Tu Than, Headwraps, Royal Robes) following historical accuracy but styled for children.

## 📐 Aspect Ratios & Orientation
- **Thumbnails (Lesson Cover):** Landscape orientation, **16:9 aspect ratio**. (Add `--ar 16:9` for Midjourney).
- **Lesson Slides:** Portrait orientation, **vertical layout (2:3 or 9:16 aspect ratio)**. (Add `--ar 2:3` for Midjourney).

## 🔡 Text in Images
- **Content:** Include a short, child-friendly Vietnamese caption (1-4 words).
- **Font:** Playful, rounded, and highly readable font style.
- **Placement:** Clear and visible, typically at the top or bottom of the scene.

## 💡 Master Style Prefix
Always use this prefix at the beginning of your prompt:
> `Children's book illustration, flat vector style, sunlit gold and emerald color palette, clean outlines, storybook aesthetic, friendly atmosphere, Vietnamese history context.`

## 🚫 Avoid
- Dark, gloomy, or violent scenes.
- Realistic, detailed textures.
- Complex cinematic lighting.
- Modern elements.
